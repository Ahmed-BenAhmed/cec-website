About Us

Learn about our journey, mission and the team behind CEC Club

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Computer Engineering Club (CEC) started as a small group of passionate students with a shared vision: creating a vibrant tech community within ENSA Berrechid. What began with just a handful of members has rapidly grown into one of the most active and influential student tech organizations on campus.

Today, we provide a platform for students to develop technical skills, collaborate on innovative projects, and connect with industry professionals.

Our Mission

Empowering students through technology, innovation, and collaboration

Educate

We provide workshops, courses, and tutorials to help students develop valuable technical skills outside the traditional curriculum.

Connect

We create opportunities for networking among students, faculty, and industry professionals through events and collaborations.

Innovate

We encourage members to pursue creative solutions to real-world problems through project-based learning and hackathons.

Support

We foster an inclusive community where everyone can find mentorship, resources, and encouragement to achieve their tech goals.

What We Do

Explore the various activities and initiatives we organize

Workshops & Courses

We regularly host hands-on workshops covering topics like web development, mobile apps, AI, cybersecurity, and more.

Competitions

We organize and participate in coding competitions, hackathons, and tech challenges to foster innovation and teamwork.

Conferences

Our speaker series brings industry experts and academics to share insights on emerging technologies and career paths.

Projects

We facilitate collaborative projects that solve real problems while building practical experience for members.

Networking

Our mixers and industry visits help students build connections that lead to internships and job opportunities.

Mentorship

We pair experienced members with newcomers to provide guidance, support, and knowledge transfer.
